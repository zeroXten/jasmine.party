Jasmine's fairy storytale

Once there was a pretty fairy in a pretty tree and she got stuck. The fairy was a good one.
She flew everywhere and she had beautiful wings. She got stuck because she wasn't looking
where she was going. Oh dear. Unfortunately, both of her beautiful wings got broken when
she got stuck in the tree. Her friend helped her get out from the tree and they went to the
fairy doctor. The fairy got better and learned to look where she was going. The end.
